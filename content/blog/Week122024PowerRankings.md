 ### Fantasy Football Weekly Recap

#### Game of Jones vs. Hurts Donit

In a thrilling matchup, Hurts Donit emerged victorious over Game of Jones with a close score of 158.26 to 151.92. Both teams exceeded their projected scores, with Game of Jones projected at 139.36 and Hurts Donit at 133.88.

**Key Performers:**
- **Hurts Donit:** Jaylen Waddle was a standout performer, scoring 28.4 points, significantly surpassing his projection of 10.49. DJ Moore also delivered an impressive performance with 26.9 points against a projection of 11.06.
- **Game of Jones:** Terry McLaurin outperformed expectations, scoring 21.2 points over a projection of 13.16. However, Deebo Samuel Sr. underperformed, managing only 3.1 points against a projection of 12.11.

**Bench Insights:**
- On the bench, Jayden Daniels of Hurts Donit scored 30.4 points, outperforming starting quarterback Jalen Hurts, who scored 15.06.

#### Shippin Up To Austin vs. Allen & Co.

Shippin Up To Austin secured a win over Allen & Co. with a score of 155.58 to 138.48. Shippin Up To Austin slightly exceeded their projected score of 151.09, while Allen & Co. met their projection almost exactly.

**Key Performers:**
- **Shippin Up To Austin:** George Kittle was a surprise star, scoring 20.2 points, well above his projection of 11.52. Tony Pollard also excelled with 21.9 points, surpassing his 14.11 projection.
- **Allen & Co.:** Josh Jacobs was a powerhouse, scoring 28.6 points, far exceeding his projected 16.15.

**Bench Insights:**
- Tua Tagovailoa on Allen & Co.'s bench delivered an impressive 28.48 points, outperforming starting quarterback Josh Allen, who did not play.

#### Final Deztination vs. Comeback Cardinals

Final Deztination narrowly defeated Comeback Cardinals with a score of 158.12 to 147.7, despite being projected at 141.67 compared to Comeback Cardinals' 155.05.

**Key Performers:**
- **Final Deztination:** Courtland Sutton was a standout with 29.7 points, greatly exceeding his projection of 13.3. Keenan Allen, despite being on the bench, scored 23.6 points, outperforming several starters.
- **Comeback Cardinals:** Trey McBride was a top performer, scoring 25.3 points against a projection of 12.86. Jordan Addison, on the bench, scored 30.2 points, outperforming starting wide receiver Justin Jefferson, who scored only 4.7.

**Bench Insights:**
- The bench performance of Jordan Addison was noteworthy, as his 30.2 points could have significantly impacted the starting lineup.

#### Red Zone Rockets vs. OC Gang

In a decisive victory, Red Zone Rockets dominated OC Gang with a score of 198.16 to 91.66, far exceeding their projected score of 150.53.

**Key Performers:**
- **Red Zone Rockets:** Saquon Barkley was the star of the match, scoring an astounding 46.2 points, far surpassing his projection of 18.28. Patrick Mahomes also shone brightly with 28.76 points against a 17.25 projection.
- **OC Gang:** Bucky Irving, on the bench, scored 27.1 points, outperforming all starting running backs.

**Bench Insights:**
- The bench performance of Bucky Irving was a missed opportunity for OC Gang, as his 27.1 points could have provided a significant boost to the starting lineup.

This week in fantasy football showcased thrilling matchups and standout performances, with several players exceeding expectations and others providing valuable insights from the bench. As the season progresses, team managers will look to optimize their lineups based on these performances and insights.
