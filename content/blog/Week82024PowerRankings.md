**Hurts Donit vs. Allen & Co.**

In a closely contested matchup, Allen & Co. edged out Hurts Donit with a score of 167.72 to 157.26. Hurts Donit, despite a strong showing, fell short of their 153.76 projection. Jalen Hurts was a standout performer, scoring 35.14 points against a 19.55 projection. On the bench, Chris Olave scored 18.7 points, outperforming starting wide receiver Jaylen Waddle, who managed only 8.5 points.

**Comeback Cardinals vs. OC Gang**

The Comeback Cardinals secured a win over OC Gang with a score of 151.68 to 134.1, despite being projected for 159.31. The Cardinals were bolstered by the performances of Trey McBride and Kyler Murray. McBride scored 21.4 points, significantly exceeding his 11.82 projection, while Murray delivered 22.18 points against an 18.12 projection.

OC Gang, projected to score 159.08, fell short despite strong performances from Jonathan Taylor and Cade Otton. Taylor scored 18.7 points, surpassing his 16.6 projection, while Otton delivered an impressive 29.1 points against a 10.54 projection. However, Davante Adams underperformed, scoring just 9.4 points against a 16.24 projection. Recent news suggests Adams is adjusting to changes in team dynamics, which may have affected his output.
