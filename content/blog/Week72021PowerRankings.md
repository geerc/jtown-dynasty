##Highlights:

**OC Gang**: OC gang squeaks past Game of Jones this week. Both teams are incredibility close in both Power Score and Team Value, but OC Gang gets the slight edge overall. Whoever score more next week will probably take this spot.

**Da Coldest In the Game**: Da Coldest has been unable to recover from their first loss a couple weeks ago to Team Ger. This team has come down from where they started the year, but they're basically a lock for the playoffs so they have time to put it back together.

**Team Ger**: Last weeks ranking was more a result of bye week bias than anything and now that that has been removed from the model Team Ger finds themselves back in 4th.

**Final Deztination**: The only way to put bye week players back into the team value calcuations also brought injured players with them. This means Final Deztination gets CMC back in the team value. It wasn't enough to push them into 4th this week, but a couple good weeks could easily push them over Team Ger.
