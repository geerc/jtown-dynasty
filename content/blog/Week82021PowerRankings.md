##Highlights:

**Game of Jones**: After sitting in second for most of this year, Game of Jones finally sits atop the league. We'll see how long they stay there.

**Da Coldest In the Game**: Da Coldest once again hold the highest team value which, along with a strong week this week, moves them back up to 2nd in the rankings. More consistency from Tyler Lockett could be huge for this team.

**OC Gang**: Another shaky week for this team, but they also lost Devante Adams to COVID for the week. With strong weeks from both Game of Jones and Da Coldest they were inevitable to drop.

**Final Deztination**: This team has performed very well without Christian McCaffrey. The teams values (currently 3rd in the league) is still getting the McCaffrey bumb, but they've proven to be a playoff contender even without him.

**Team Ger**: This team was lucky to scrape out a win this week to stay just 1 game back from OC Gang. This team better turn it around for the big divisional game this week.

**Red Zone Rockets**: Despite Patrick Mahomes playing like the JV backup, the Red Zone Rockets put up a good week and lifted themselves out of the cellar. There's a long way to 5th though so it's unlikely for them to make any more jumps.
