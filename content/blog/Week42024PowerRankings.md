**Fantasy Football Weekly Recap: Thrilling Matchups and Surprising Performances**

**Shippin Up To Austin Dominates Hurts Donit**

In a decisive victory, Shippin Up To Austin outperformed Hurts Donit with a final score of 162.42 to 124.62. Despite being projected to score 147.99, Shippin Up To Austin exceeded expectations, while Hurts Donit fell short of their 144.98 projection.

Key players for Shippin Up To Austin included George Kittle, who scored 14.5 points, outperforming his projected 12.73. Mike Evans was another standout, scoring 23.4 points against a projection of 14.58. A notable bench performance came from D'Andre Swift, who scored 29.5 points, significantly higher than any starting RB on the team.

Hurts Donit saw mixed performances from their starting lineup. Bijan Robinson underperformed with 11.4 points against a projection of 20.12, while James Conner exceeded his projection with 18.3 points. Notably, David Montgomery, who was benched, scored 15.0 points, outperforming Bijan Robinson in the starting lineup.

**Final Deztination Prevails Over Team Ger**

In another compelling matchup, Final Deztination triumphed over Team Ger with a score of 149.24 to 119.8. Final Deztination slightly exceeded their projected 144.02, while Team Ger significantly underperformed their 156.97 projection.

Final Deztination's Alvin Kamara was a key contributor, scoring 24.9 points, well above his projected 18.97. Travis Kelce also outperformed expectations with 15.9 points against a projection of 10.47. On the bench, Jake Ferguson scored 11.9 points, which was higher than the starting TE's performance.

Team Ger had a disappointing performance from Josh Allen, who scored only 7.3 points against a projection of 21.45. However, CeeDee Lamb was a bright spot, scoring 23.6 points, surpassing his projection of 19.43. Chuba Hubbard on the bench scored 22.1 points, outperforming all starting RBs.

**Comeback Cardinals Crush Red Zone Rockets**

In a high-scoring affair, Comeback Cardinals defeated Red Zone Rockets with a staggering 195.88 to 117.28. The Comeback Cardinals exceeded their projection of 160.59, while Red Zone Rockets fell short of their 153.47 projection.

Derrick Henry led the Comeback Cardinals with an impressive 35.9 points, far exceeding his projection of 15.3. Nico Collins also shone, scoring 33.1 points against a projection of 17.39. On the bench, Jordan Addison scored 22.9 points, outperforming several starting WRs.

Red Zone Rockets had a lackluster performance from Breece Hall, who scored only 3.8 points against a projection of 19.92. Diontae Johnson was a rare highlight, scoring 21.3 points, surpassing his 15.2 projection. Patrick Mahomes, who was benched, scored 13.0 points, which was higher than the starting QB Joe Burrow's 16.28 points.

**Game of Jones Edges Out OC Gang**

In a closely contested matchup, Game of Jones narrowly defeated OC Gang with a final score of 158.78 to 150.8. Game of Jones exceeded their projection of 140.77, while OC Gang fell just short of their 147.95 projection.

Baker Mayfield was a standout for Game of Jones, scoring 28.88 points against a projection of 16.52. Terry McLaurin also exceeded expectations with 18.2 points against a projection of 13.46. On the bench, Jordan Love scored 28.16 points, outperforming the starting QB Baker Mayfield.

For OC Gang, Kenneth Walker III was a top performer, scoring 33.6 points, significantly higher than his projection of 13.43. Jonathan Taylor also impressed with 19.8 points against a projection of 17.93. However, Travis Etienne Jr. underperformed with only 7.0 points against a projection of 15.29.
