 **Fantasy Football Week Recap: Thrilling Matchups and Surprising Performances**

In an exhilarating week of fantasy football, teams battled fiercely, with some matchups coming down to the wire. Let's dive into the highlights and noteworthy performances from this week's games.

**Red Zone Rockets vs. Hurts Donit**

In a closely contested matchup, the Red Zone Rockets emerged victorious over Hurts Donit with a final score of 157.12 to 133.46, surpassing their projected score of 138.33. The Rockets' triumph was fueled by standout performances from Joe Mixon, who scored 27.2 points, significantly outperforming his projection of 16.85. Meanwhile, A.J. Brown also shone brightly, scoring 23.6 points against a projection of 15.2. On the bench, Patrick Mahomes, surprisingly, did not contribute any points, as he remained unutilized this week.

For Hurts Donit, Bijan Robinson was a beacon of hope, scoring 25.5 points, exceeding his projection of 18.21. However, the team's overall performance fell short of their projected 142.57. Notably, Chris Olave underperformed significantly, scoring -0.5 points against a projection of 11.48. On the bench, Jalen Hurts delivered a solid 21.86 points, which could have bolstered the starting lineup.

**Game of Jones vs. Allen & Co.**

In another nail-biter, Allen & Co. narrowly defeated Game of Jones with a score of 154.8 to 149.12. Allen & Co. closely matched their projected score of 151.95, thanks to Chris Godwin's explosive performance, scoring 35.5 points, far exceeding his projection of 15.76. Josh Allen also played a crucial role, contributing 24.4 points.

Game of Jones, despite the loss, saw impressive performances from Terry McLaurin, who scored 23.3 points against a projection of 16.73, and Cole Kmet, who delivered a remarkable 24.0 points, surpassing his projection of 7.3. On the bench, Mark Andrews scored 15.6 points, which could have been a valuable addition to the starting lineup.

**Shippin Up To Austin vs. Comeback Cardinals**

Shippin Up To Austin dominated the Comeback Cardinals with a decisive 163.02 to 134.66 victory, closely aligning with their projected score of 162.96. George Kittle was a standout performer, scoring 22.8 points against a projection of 14.73. Additionally, D'Andre Swift impressed with 21.9 points, outperforming his projection of 16.99.

The Comeback Cardinals struggled to meet their projected score of 138.71, despite Derrick Henry's stellar 25.2-point performance, which exceeded his projection of 18.2. Kyler Murray, however, underperformed with 11.96 points against a projection of 17.78. On the bench, Jordan Addison remained unutilized, scoring no points.

**Final Deztination vs. OC Gang**

In a one-sided affair, OC Gang overwhelmed Final Deztination with a commanding 169.48 to 97.92 victory. OC Gang surpassed their projected score of 150.62, with Garrett Wilson leading the charge, scoring 24.7 points against a projection of 16.63. Kenneth Walker III also excelled, contributing 20.9 points, exceeding his projection of 17.49.

Final Deztination struggled to reach their projected score of 146.79, with Dak Prescott notably underperforming, scoring only 3.22 points against a projection of 18.14. On the bench, Aaron Rodgers delivered 17.76 points, which could have bolstered the lineup. Zay Flowers, however, was a bright spot, scoring 22.4 points against a projection of 14.01.
